Poetry Therapy For Life's Journey

Edited by Geri Giebel Chavis & Lila Lizabeth Weisberger



"The Healing Fountain" is an introduction to the emerging practice of Poetry Therapy. Offering case studies, examples and open-ended discussions, it is designed to be a valuable resource not only for poetry therapists and trainees in poetry therapy but also for educators, mental health professionals, creative artists, as well as everyday lovers of memorable, moving poetry who are engaged in their own privately led journeys of personal discovery.



Co-editor Lila Lizabeth Weisberger is the former President of the National Association of Poetry Therapy, the leading organization for this growing and innovative field.
