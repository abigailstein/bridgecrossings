It can be very therapeutic to choose segments of poems and quotes when you are working through an issue.
Ultimately, it helps you find your own voice. This is a very simple exercise to do and there is great release in this way of expression and it is fun too.

Said one oyster to a neighboring oyster, "l have a very great pain within me. It is heavy and round and I am in distress." And the other oyster replied with haughty complacence "Praise be to the heavens and to the sea, I have no pain within me. I am well and whole both within and without." At that moment a crab was passing by and heard the two oysters, and he said to the one who was well and whole both within and without, "Yes, you are well and whole, but the pain that your neighbor bears is a pearl of exceeding beauty."
