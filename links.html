The National Association For Poetry Therapy, NAPT is an organization with members throughout the United States. The International membership is growing with members in Japan, South Africa, Taiwan, Ireland, Israel, Gemany and Finland. The annual conferences are inspirational and educational. For information check the web site of NAPT, or e-mail rdaniel@his.com. The Poet Laureate of the United States, Robert Pinsky has created a program entitled The Favorite Poem Project. Robert Pinsky and his team have selected favorite poems of Americans for a book and video. April is National Poetry Month and is celebrated in various ways. The Favorite Poem website.
